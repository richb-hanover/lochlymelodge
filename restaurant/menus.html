Menus — 2017

Menus at the Restaurant

The Restaurant at Loch Lyme Lodge is open to the public. Here are sample menus for all our meals:

Breakfast Menu: Enjoy a full or continental breakfast every morning.

Lunch Bar Menu: We offer a range of sandwiches, soups, salads, ice cream, and treats.

Daily Dinner Menu: Thursday through Saturday, we serve a full dinner in the dining room.

Nightly Dinner Specials: In addition to our daily dinner menu, we also offer a special theme each evening.

  • Prime Rib Night menu Saturdays, we always serve Prime Rib Menu
  • Pizza Night Menu Build your own pizza, or enjoy our other entráes.
  • New Orleans Night Menu Try out our NOLA fare
  • Italian Night Menu Inspired by Italy's fare

Buffet Dinner Menus: On Sunday and Wednesday evenings, we have an outdoor buffet by the pond, with music from a local band.

  • Sunday Buffet Menu
  • Wednesday Cookout Menu

Reservations: It's always a good idea to make a reservation, especially for the buffet. Please contact our Innkeepers at LochLymeLodge@gmail.com or call us at 603-795-2141. We look forward to seeing you!

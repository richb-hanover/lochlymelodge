Cabin Rates

Housekeeping Cabin Rates 2016

The weekly rate for the housekeeping cabins includes lodging, the use of all Loch Lyme facilities, daily firewood delivery and trash collection, and linens. It does not include meals. Fresh linens are delivered on Wednesdays and Saturdays and are available upon request at other times. A typical housekeeping cabin has a living room with a fireplace, from one to four bedrooms, a bath, a kitchen or kitchenette, an outdoor campground grill, and a screened porch. Most housekeeping cabins have an additional daybed in the living room. The number of guests per cabin is listed below. We regret that sleeping bag accommodations are not available.

Housekeeping guests are invited to enjoy the meals offered in the Loch Lyme dining rooms, the Sunday night lakeside buffet, and the takeout lunch bar.Advance reservations are suggested.

The housekeeping units are popular and may be fully booked, for some midsummer weeks, two years in advance. However, our charts change frequently as families arrange summer vacation plans around school schedules, summer camps, or family weddings. We keep a wait list in case of cancellations, and always attempt to offer guests their first choice of accommodations. For last minute reservations we will rent cabins with kitchens by the night on the bed and breakfast plan if they are available.

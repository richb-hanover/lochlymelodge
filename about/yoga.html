Yoga Workshop — 2017

Midweek Yoga Workshop - August 2018

Enjoy four days and three nights at Loch Lyme Lodge with two yoga sessions per day. The workshop runs from Monday through Friday, August 27 to 31. In addition to your yoga practice, enjoy swimming, kayaking, boating by our lakeside waterfront, or hike on our grounds, or simply relax in the beauty of a rural setting.

Reservations: To reserve the Yoga Workshop package, book directly with our Innkeepers at LochLymeLodge@gmail.com or call us at 603-795-2141

The Details

  • Please bring your own mat, strap, and props if you have them. Otherwise, call ahead and we will arrange to provide them.
  • Cost includes yoga session fee and lodging. $350 per person or $550 per couple.
  • Cabin lodging is for three nights with continental breakfast each morning. All linens and towels are provided. All cabins have a mini-fridge, fireplace, and daily firewood delivery.
  • Please feel free to use the facilities if you arrive before the check-in time, or after you check out.
  • We recommend a relaxed dinner in the Lodge. Order from an à la carte menu by our wonderful Chef Janet. Reservations required — BYOB
  • Our Innkeepers, Jay and Amy Kelly, can provide information about activities here at the Lodge and in the area. Contact them at 603-795-2141, or LochLymeLodge@gmail.com

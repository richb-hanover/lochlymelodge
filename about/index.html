Online Reservations

We are pleased to offer online reservations. Click on the link to check availability and to reserve your cabin.

CHECK-IN, CHECK-OUT

Check-in time is 3:00 PM. If you arrive early and your cabin is ready, you are welcome to check in early. If a wait is necessary, you can change in the restroom located downstairs in the Main Lodge before heading to the lake. If you plan to arrive later than 8:00 PM, please contact us.

Check-out time from cabins is 10:00 AM. On the day of your departure you are invited to continue to enjoy the property after you check out.

CANCELLATIONS/REFUNDS

If you cancel twelve or more weeks ahead of your arrival date, we will issue a full refund, minus a $30 service charge. If you cancel less than 12 weeks prior to your arrival day, we will return your deposit, less the $30 service charge, only if we are able to rebook the cabin.

Note that the Wedding Cancellation Policy differs.

NO SMOKING

We have a strict no smoking policy in the Main Lodge and the cabins all year.

2018 RESERVATIONS

We are accepting reservations for the 2018 season. We ask for a $100 deposit upon booking. We will begin taking reservations for 2019 in the spring of 2018.

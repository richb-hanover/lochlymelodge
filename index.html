Begin a Family Tradition with us at Loch Lyme Lodge

Loch Lyme Lodge is situated in Lyme, NH, not far from Hanover and Dartmouth College, in the picturesque Connecticut River Valley. Our mix of 21 bed and breakfast and housekeeping cabins are set in 120 acres of fields and woodlands, a peaceful and natural setting that makes Loch Lyme Lodge a wonderful place for a family reunion, family camp or a simple quiet vacation.

The Lodge is a rustic and eco-friendly vacation spot. Our furnishings are simple and comfortable; we have fireplaces and games, not televisions or video arcades. We have screened porches instead of air conditioning, and for entertainment, guests and staff often gather in the barn in the evenings. We provide Internet access in the main Lodge building.

Our cabins can accommodate anything from one to two people to large parties. Decide how many people will be staying, whether you want bed and breakfast lodging or housekeeping cabins - we can find a perfect cabin in the trees or by the lake.

Loch Lyme Lodge is a wonderful place for weddings. Use the full property, and treat your guests to all our amenities.

Our dining room is open to Lodge guests and the public. We offer full breakfast, sandwiches and snacks during the day, and our chef prepares a wonderful dinner in the evenings. Our Sunday buffet and Wednesday cookout are complemented by our Summer Music Series.

We know you have a choice of cabins in New Hampshire and hope you will consider us for your next vacation. We look forward to speaking with you soon!

Amy & Jay Kelly

About the Lodge and Cabins

B&B and Housekeeping Cabins

Loch Lyme Lodge has 21 rustic cabins, a mix of Bed and Breakfast and Housekeeping cabins.

Use the menus above to find the perfect cabin for you. Choose by cabin size or use the map to find the right location for you if accessibility is a concern.

Bed and Breakfast Cabins

A full American breakfast, regular maid service, the use of all Loch Lyme facilities, and daily firewood delivery and trash collection and linens are all part of staying in a B&B cabin. A typical B&B cabin has a bedroom with a king bed or two single beds, a living room with a fireplace and a daybed, a bath, and a porch. Housekeeping Cabins

The Housekeeping Cabins include lodging, the use of all Loch Lyme facilities, daily firewood delivery and trash collection, and linens. The weekly rate does not include meals. A typical Housekeeping cabin has a living room with a fireplace, from one to four bedrooms, a bath, a kitchen or kitchenette, an outdoor campground grill, and a screened porch. Dining at Loch Lyme Lodge

The dining room at Loch Lyme Lodge is open to Lodge guests and the public.

We serve a full American breakfast each morning.

Come in your bathing suit to our Lunch Bar and find salads, sandwiches, soup and ice cream.

We offer full dinner service on Thursday through Saturday evenings. After soup and/or salad, you can select from one of our meat, fish or poultry or vegetarian options. Take a look at our sample menu on the Restaurant page. We always have desserts.

Our traditional Sunday night buffet is served by the lake at 6:00 starting on June 25. And we're expanding our offerings with a Wednesday Cookout with chicken, sausages, burgers, vegetarian options, salad, and desserts from 5:30 to 7:30pm.

Please remember that we do not have a liquor license, so feel free to bring your own wine or beer. If you're not staying in a cabin, we appreciate a call ahead to make a reservation, 603-795-2141. Please check our Restaurant page for more details.

Built in 1784

The Lodge, built in 1784, is home to our dining rooms and kitchen headed by our own chef. Our dining room is open to Lodge guests and the public. We offer full breakfast, sandwiches and snacks during the day, and our chef prepares a wonderful dinner in the evenings. Don’t miss our Sunday buffet.

The Lake

Relax by our Private Waterfront

The waterfront is a popular area for guests and families. Bordered by a grassy area dotted with lawn chairs, stairs lead down to a dock, a swimming area with a sandy bottom, and a raft. Boats, kayaks, and canoes are available for use by guests. The lake is also a great fishing spot.

A waterfront buffet takes place every Sunday.

A Pet Friendly Vacation

We know that your pet needs a vacation too, and our cabins are pet friendly, so feel free to bring them.

We enjoy being able to continue the long tradition of offering pet-friendly lodging. We ask that you please bring a leash, keep your pets off the cabin furniture and always pick up after toileting your pet (we can supply plastic bags if needed). We have a swimming area specially created for your dog to cool off and have fun. We ask that dogs be kept out of the main swimming area and stairway into pond. Only guide dogs are allowed in the main lodge.

A Green Vacation

In more ways than one..

If you are looking for a eco-friendly vacation, you have come to the right place. We have always been environmentally friendly here. The cabins themselves are rustic and use minimal electricity, and all have fireplaces. We use CFL’s and LED’s and even dry your sheets outside when we can. We have our own small garden and buy first from local farms for our restaurant. We recycle and compost.

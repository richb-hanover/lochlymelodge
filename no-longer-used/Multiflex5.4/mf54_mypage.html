Family Vacation Traditions

Begin one with us at Loch Lyme Lodge

We offer a wonderful space for a family reunion, weddings, or a simple quiet vacation, with an emphasis on family fun, peace and quiet, a caring staff and a congenial clientele. Loch Lyme Lodge is a rustic vacation spot. To us this means simple furnishings with no telephones or televisions, no video arcade, no hot tubs, no microwaves and no bar with nightly entertainment. (We do, though, provide Internet access in the main house.) Please read the Cabin Descriptions, and look through the Photo Gallery. Our dining room is open to Lodge guests and the public. We offer full breakfast, sandwiches and snacks during the day, and our chef Ron Perry always prepares a wonderful dinner in the evenings. Finally, contact us if you have any questions or want more information. We hope to speak with you soon!

About the Lodge and Cabins

The Historic Lodge

Built in 1784

The Lodge, built in 1784, is home to our dining rooms and kitchen headed by our chef Ron Perry. Our dining room is open to Lodge guests and the public. We offer full breakfast, sandwiches and snacks during the day, and our chef always prepares a wonderful dinner in the evenings.

Dining

We serve a full American breakfast each morning. At lunch you can come in your bathing suit and find salads, sandwiches, soup and ice cream. Each evening we offer full dinners. After soup and/or salad, you can select from one of our meat, fish or poultry or vegetarian options. We always have desserts. Our traditional Sunday night buffet is served by the lake at 6:00. Take a look at our sample menu. Please remember that we do not have a liquor license, so feel free to bring your own wine. The dining room at Loch Lyme Lodge is open to Lodge guests and the public. If you're not staying in a cabin, we appreciate a call ahead to make a reservation, 603-795-2141.

21 Rustic Cabins

Bed and Breakfast or Efficiency

Loch Lyme Lodge has 11 Bed and Breakfast cabins and 10 Housekeeping cabins.

Bed and Breakfast Cabins

The Bed and Breakfast cabins include a full American breakfast as well as lodging, daily maid service, the use of all Loch Lyme facilities, and daily firewood delivery. A typical B&B cabin has a bedroom with a king bed or two single beds, a living room with a fireplace and a daybed, a bath, and a porch. Two bedroom cabins typically have a king bed in one bedroom and two single beds in the second.

Housekeeping Cabins

The Housekeeping Cabins include lodging, the use of all Loch Lyme facilities, daily firewood delivery and trash collection, and linens. The weekly rate does not include meals. A typical housekeeping cabin has a living room with a fireplace, from one to four bedrooms, a bath, a kitchen or kitchenette, an outdoor campground grill, and a screened porch. Most housekeeping cabins have an additional daybed in the living room.

The Lake

Relax by the Private Waterfront

Loch Lyme Lodge offers many possibilities for recreation. The waterfront is bordered by a grassy area dotted with lawn chairs. Stairs lead down to a dock, a swimming area with a sandy bottom, and a raft. Boats, kayaks, and canoes are available. The fishing in the lake is excellent, and some people are catching smallmouth bass. Licenses may be purchased locally.

A waterfront buffet takes place every Sunday.

A Green Vacation

In more ways then one..

If you are looking for a ecological friendly vacation then you have come to the right place. We have always been environmentally friendly here at Loch Lyme Lodge, and we believe you can green and still have fund. Vacation here and you will be leaving a small carbon footprint. The cabins themselves are rustic and use minimal electricity, and all have fireplaces. We use compact lightbulbs and even dry your sheets outside when we can. Recycling bins are provided.

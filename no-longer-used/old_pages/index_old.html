New Hampshire Cabins, Dartmouth-Lake Sunapee Area

Family Vacation Traditions

Begin one with us at Loch Lyme Lodge

Historic Loch Lyme Lodge is situated on Post Pond in Lyme,NH, not far from Hanover and Dartmouth, in the picturesque Connecticut river valley. We have 21 cabins for rent set in 120 acres of fields and woodlands. These cabins are a mixture of bed and breakfast and efficiency cabin accommodations, in a peaceful and natural setting. This makes it a wonderful place for a family reunion, family camp or a simple quiet vacation, with an emphasis on family fun, peace and quiet, a caring staff and a congenial clientele. We are also able to host Weddings.

Loch Lyme Lodge is a rustic and eco-friendly vacation spot. This means simple furnishings with no telephones or televisions, no video arcade, no hot tubs,no air conditioning, no microwaves and no bar with nightly entertainment. We do provide Internet access in the main house.

There is no shortage of things to do as we are surrounded by lakes and mountains, and this is a great base for fishing , hiking and biking the region.

Our cabins can accomodate anything from one to two people to large parties. Decide how many people will be staying, whether you want a bed and breakfast or efficiency cabin then use this site to find your perfect NH cabin in the trees or by the lake. Our dining room is open to Lodge guests and the public. We offer full breakfast, sandwiches and snacks during the day, and our chef Ron Perry always prepares a wonderful dinner in the evenings.

Finally, we know you have a choice of cabins in New Hampshire, so we hope you will consider us for your next vacation. We hope to speak with you soon!

Amy & Jay Kelly
Innkeepers
Loch Lyme Lodge. Lyme. NH

About the Lodge and Cabins

The Historic Lodge

Built in 1784

The Lodge, built in 1784, is home to our dining rooms and kitchen headed by our chef Ron Perry. Our dining room is open to Lodge guests and the public. We offer full breakfast, sandwiches and snacks during the day, and our chef always prepares a wonderful dinner in the evenings.

Dining

We serve a full American breakfast each morning. At lunch you can come in your bathing suit and find salads, sandwiches, soup and ice cream. Each evening we offer full dinners. After soup and/or salad, you can select from one of our meat, fish or poultry or vegetarian options. We always have desserts. Our traditional Sunday night buffet is served by the lake at 6:00. Take a look at our sample menu on the dining page. Please remember that we do not have a liquor license, so feel free to bring your own wine. The dining room at Loch Lyme Lodge is open to Lodge guests and the public. If you're not staying in a cabin, we appreciate a call ahead to make a reservation, 603-795-2141.

21 Rustic Cabins

Bed and Breakfast or Efficiency

Loch Lyme Lodge has 11 Bed and Breakfast cabins and 10 Efficiency cabins.

Use the menus above to find the perfect cabin for you, you can choose by cabin size or use the map to find the right location for you if accessibility is a concern.

Bed and Breakfast Cabins

The Bed and Breakfast cabins include a full American breakfast as well as lodging, daily maid service, the use of all Loch Lyme facilities, and daily firewood delivery. A typical B&B cabin has a bedroom with a king bed or two single beds, a living room with a fireplace and a daybed, a bath, and a porch. Two bedroom cabins typically have a king bed in one bedroom and two single beds in the second.

Efficiency Cabins

The Efficiency Cabins include lodging, the use of all Loch Lyme facilities, daily firewood delivery and trash collection, and linens. The weekly rate does not include meals. A typical Efficiency cabin has a living room with a fireplace, from one to four bedrooms, a bath, a kitchen or kitchenette, an outdoor campground grill, and a screened porch. Most Efficiency cabins have an additional daybed in the living room.

The Rates Page lists the rates of our cabins.

The Lake

Relax by the Private Waterfront

The waterfront is a popular area for guests and familises. Bordered by a grassy area dotted with lawn chairs, stairs lead down to a dock, a swimming area with a sandy bottom, and a raft. Boats, kayaks, and canoes are available for use by guests. The lake is also a great fishing spot.

A waterfront buffet takes place every Sunday.

A Green Vacation

In more ways than one..

If you are looking for a eco-friendly vacation then you have come to the right place. We have always been environmentally friendly here, and we believe you can be green and still have fun. Vacation here and you will be leaving a small carbon footprint. The cabins themselves are rustic and use minimal electricity, and all have fireplaces. We use compact lightbulbs and even dry your sheets outside when we can. We always try to source fresh products from local farms for our restaurant. Recycling bins are provided.

The Loch Lyme Lodge Summer Music Series

Summer Music Series

We've expanded our music series to include two outstanding opportunities to hear local bands every week.

Come by on Sunday for our traditional Sunday Buffet. We serve from 6:00pm to 7:00pm, and the music starts shortly thereafter.
  Adults $25 Children (5-12) $10 Four and under Free

Wednesday evenings, we will serve from 5:30-7:30pm, with music running throughout.
  Adults $20 Children (5-12) $8 Four and under Free

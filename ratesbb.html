Cabin Rates

Bed and Breakfast Rates 2016

The bed and breakfast rates include a full American breakfast served from 8:00-9:30 and/or a continental breakfast bar from 9:30-10:30 as well as lodging, daily maid service, the use of all Loch Lyme facilities, and daily firewood delivery. A typical cabin has a bedroom with a king bed or two single beds, a living room with a fireplace and a daybed, a bath, and a porch. Two bedroom cabins typically have a king bed in one bedroom and two single beds in the second.

Some summer weeks book quickly, especially in late July and early August. Two bedroom cabins fill rapidly for most of July and August. We do keep a wait list in the event of cancellations and always attempt to offer guests their first choice of accommodations. Call to check availability for last minute reservations for summer getaways. (1-800-423-2141) There usually is an open room or cabin (and unlimited swimming in the lake) on a hot July or August day.

Our prices for bed and breakfast cabins depend on the number of people staying in the cabin. Children aged 4 and under are free. We also have differing rates for our one-bedroom and two-bedroom cabins.
